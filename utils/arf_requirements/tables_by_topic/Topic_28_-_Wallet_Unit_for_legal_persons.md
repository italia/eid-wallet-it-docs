# Topic 28 - Wallet Unit for legal persons

| **Requirement ID** | **Legacy ID** | **Description** | **Status** |
|-------------------|---------------|------------------|------------|
| LP_01 |  | The Commission SHALL develop a Legal-person PID Rulebook to specify the attestation scheme and other technical details applicable for legal-person PIDs. | 🟡 |
| LP_02 |  | The attestation type of a legal-person PID SHALL be different from the attestation type of a natural person PID. *Note: See [[Topic 12](./annex-2.02-high-level-requirements-by-topic.md#a239-topic-1... | 🟡 |
| LP_03 |  | A legal-person PID SHALL comply with all requirements in the Legal-person PID Rulebook mentioned in LP_01. | 🟡 |
